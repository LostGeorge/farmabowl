Our Vision...

Sustainability


Here at Farm-a-Bowl, we use top-notch, high-quality ingredients for every one of our bowls to support our local scene and provide a healthy experience for both our customers and our community. We’ve reached out to local farmers in Oregon to get the freshest, most natural ingredients. We believe in using USDA certified organic foods to ensure that all of our ingredients contain no artificial ingredients and meet proper environmental standards. With proper farming techniques and healthy ingredients, we ensure that our ingredients will remain sustainable while we provide nourishing food to help sustain our customers.

Accessibility


With over 30 locations over the state of Oregon, we have drivers ready to deliver to any city around Central Oregon and Willamette Valley. Our bowls are designed to withstand any season and any weather. Rather than pouring our resources into hot food that may lose its quality through delivery, we chose to optimize our costs by focusing on our ingredients and crafting simply, masterful bowls that stay chilled from our kitchen to your house. We strive to provide busy customers with a convenient, first-rate culinary experience for half the price and none of the hassle.

Dependability


Our timely delivery system guarantees that you will receive your bowl within 30 minutes to ensure that our bowls retain their quality and freshness. With ingredients straight from local farms, no nutritional value is lost through either premature harvesting or flash freezing. Our bowls maintain their quality and flavor due to our efficient distribution method combined with our chilled production process. This way, with any bowl you order, you can always look forward to the great taste of our bowls.

Enjoyability


Whether you’re ordering for yourself, your co-workers, your vegan friend, or the whole family, here at Farm-a-Bowl, we’ve created countless options to suit everyone’s tastes. Rather than being limited to one cuisine type, our chefs have innovated new and creative recipes that derive culinary inspirations from cultures all around the world. Whether it be our Sockeye Salmon Poke Bowl, a Hawaiian, Asian-inspired local classic, our Smoked Sausage Charcuterie Bowl with French roots, or our vegan SuperFood Quinoa Bowl, we have delicious and healthy options for everyone.
